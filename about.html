Meet the team

Jessica Lambe

I graduated from Hartpury University with a 1st Class Honours degree in Sports Therapy in the summer of 2023. My experience at university in the Student Clinic and my placement at Royal Wootton Bassett RFC allowed me to see and treat an array of acute and chronic musculoskeletal injuries. My degree has armed me with the skills and knowledge to provide well-rounded treatment including injury assessment, initial first aid, complete injury management, joint mobilisations and exercise prescription.

Further to this I completed a qualification in Sports Massage to ensure that I was equipped with sufficient skills to provide all-round effective treatment. Since graduating from university I have run a Sports Massage Clinic at The Broad Street Practice in Stamford.

My qualifications include:

  • BSc (Hons) Sports Therapy - First Class
  • ITEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Sports Massage
  • Member of the Society of Sports Therapists
